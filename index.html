Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some common questions we receive from customers regarding our waterproofing services. If you have further queries, feel free to contact us!

What is waterproofing, and why is it important?

Waterproofing is the process of applying materials and techniques to prevent water from penetrating a building. It protects your property from water damage, mold, and structural issues.

How do I know if my property needs waterproofing?

If you notice signs like water stains on walls, mold, damp odors, or cracks in the foundation, your property likely needs waterproofing.

What areas of my building need waterproofing?

Common areas include basements, roofs, foundations, bathrooms, terraces, and balconies—places exposed to water or moisture.

How much does waterproofing cost?

The cost depends on the area, materials, and complexity. We provide free consultations and estimates based on your specific needs.

What types of waterproofing do you offer?

We offer foundation waterproofing, roof waterproofing, basement sealing, bathroom waterproofing, and more using techniques like membrane waterproofing and liquid applications.
